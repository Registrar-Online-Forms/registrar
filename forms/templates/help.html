Help

What is this website?

This website is a portal to access online versions of all the forms used by the Registrar at Benedictine College. This portal allows you to easily submit forms to the to the Registrar or professors without the previous hassle of finding them in person or emailing them.

How do I create a form?

There are many ways to create a form.
On the top right corner of the page, you can click the "New Form" button and select the form you want to fill out.
Alternatively, you can go to the New Form page. You can also find this page by clicking the dropdown menue in the top left corner and selecting "New Form". This will bring you to a page listing all the types of forms you can fill out.

How can I view a form I submitted?

You can go to the History page to view the status of every form you submitted. Clicking on specific forms will bring up a detailed overview of its contents.

How will I know when my form has been signed?

You will recieve an email when your professor has signed the form and submitted it to the Registrar to review.

How do I fill out a form?

Follow the promptings of the text boxes and enter all the appropriate information into the form.
If you cannot find something, you can look in RavenZone under "Student Self-Service", or ask your advisor for help. When you are done, click "Submit" and the form will automatically be sent to the appropriate person.

Who is my advisor?

You can find out who your advidior is through RavenZone.

Step by step:
Log in to RavenZone.com.
Click "Student Planning" link under "Student Self-Service" section.
Click "Student Planning".
Click "Go to Plan & Schedule".
At the top, click "Advising" from the tabs.
There you will find your advisors.
