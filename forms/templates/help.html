Help

What is this website?

This website contains online versions of all the forms used by the Registrar at Benedictine College. It allows you to easily submit forms to the correct people without having to track them down or figure out who to send it to.
This website was created by Joe Bieker, Colleen Doran, Ben Maruco, and Daniel Wee as their Senior Capstone project. (Cosider moving the credits note to the bottom but I don't feel like it right now -Ben)

How do I create a form?

There are several ways to create a form. You can click the New Form button in the top right corner and select the form you want to fill out, or you can navigate to the New Form page by clicking this link or by selecting New Form from the dropdown menu in the top left corner. Then select the form you want to fill out.

How can I view a form I submitted?

You can go to the History page to view the status of every form you submitted. Then you can click on a form to view it.

How will I know when my form has been signed?

You will recieve an email when your professor has signed the form and submitted it to the Registrar to review.

How do I fill out a form?

Simply enter all the appropriate information into the form. If you can not find something, you can look in RavenZone under "Student Self-Service", or ask your advisor for help. When you are done, click Submit and the form will automatically be sent to the appropriate person.
